Developed a lightweight intrusion detection system leveraging autoencoder neural networks to detect hardware Trojans in FPGA bitstreams. Achieved **91% accuracy** with **AUC 0.96**, and optimized for embedded use with **<0.021s latency** and **1.14 MiB memory**.
